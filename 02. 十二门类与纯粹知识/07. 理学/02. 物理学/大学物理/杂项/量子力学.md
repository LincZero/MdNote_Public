# 薛定谔方程

一维薛定谔方程
$$
-\frac{h^2}{2\mu}
	\frac{\partial^2\psi(x,t)}{\partial x^2}
+U(x,t)\psi(x,t)
=ih\frac{\partial\psi(x,t)}{\partial t}
$$
三维薛定谔方程
$$
-\frac{h^2}{2\mu}(
	\frac{\partial^2\psi}{\partial x^2}
	+\frac{\partial^2\psi}{\partial y^2}
	+\frac{\partial^2\psi}{\partial z^2}
)
+U(x,y,z)\psi
=ih\frac{\partial\psi}{\partial t}
$$
定态薛定谔方程
$$
-\frac{h^2}{2\mu}\triangledown ^2\psi
+U\psi
=E\psi
$$
